NIT PATNA

Until 2015 the college had two separate festivals, the cultural fest Melange and the technical fest Corona. From 2015 onwards the college celebrates Corona and Melange together, occurring every year in January. The event is entirely managed by students.Students from colleges all over the country participate including students from IIT Patna, BIT Patna, NIT Jamshedpur, Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology, Rajasthan College of Engineering for Women, Arya Institute of Engineering and Technology, Bengal College of Engineering and Technology, NIT Tripura and NIT Mizoram. Some events also invite students from local schools such St. Michael's High School, Patna, Loyola High School, Patna and Don Bosco Academy.

Corona And Melange is the official Techfest of NIT Patna and the grandest event held in NIT Patna

Why come to TCF’19?

Techfest provides a platform for one and all to witness one of the most beautiful and groundbreaking amalgamations of science, technology and art with pure delight and enthusiasm. With a display of motivating orations, state of the art technology, cut-throat competitions and breathtaking performances for its audience, Corona And Melange for the past 3 years has served to be the ideal destination for the millennials to learn, create and experience the beauty of technology and arts.

About TCF

TCF’19 is the Annual Techno-cultural Fest to garner the young innovative budding technocrats and entrepreneurs. The motto of this event is to inculcate a creative atmosphere in the campus and thus encouraging the young minds to fetch both, innovative ideas and solutions for the problems which stand as a challenge in the spirit of science and technology and thus push forward the wheel of progress. It’s a national level techno-cultural fest which aims at serving as a platform for all the interested students to showcase their skill and creativity. Colleges from all over the India participate in this national level fest enhancing a pure interactive atmosphere particularly during the fest. During the fest, plethora of technical and cultural events is organized along with a blend of various fun events, keeping up the tempo of this grand event. We invite eminent personalities for guest lectures to interact with the students and hence encourage them and keep the fire ignited within their hearts to work for the betterment of the nation and hence the mankind.
